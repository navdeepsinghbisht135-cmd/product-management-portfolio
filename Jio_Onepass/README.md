# Jio OnePass – Hypothetical Product Case Study

A unified, tiered loyalty program concept designed for the Jio ecosystem to solve fragmentation across commerce, telecom, OTT, and payments.

---

## Overview
Jio OnePass is a conceptual product that connects multiple Jio platforms (JioMart, AJIO, Reliance Digital, JioCinema/JioHotstar, MyJio, JioPay) into a single loyalty and rewards system.

It introduces:
- Unified rewards wallet (Jio Points)
- Tiered membership model (Lite, Plus, Max)
- Cross-platform earning & redemption
- Family pooling (Max tier)

---

## Problem Statement
Despite owning multiple high-scale digital platforms, Jio operates in silos:

- No unified loyalty system
- No cross-platform rewards
- Low ecosystem stickiness
- Weak top-of-mind recall during purchase decisions

This limits cross-sell, retention, and overall customer lifetime value.

---

## Proposed Solution
Jio OnePass creates a single loyalty layer across the ecosystem:

- One rewards currency (Jio Points)
- One membership system across all apps
- Tier-based benefits to drive monetization
- Telecom-led onboarding via MyJio

---

## Key Features

### 1. Tiered Membership
- Lite (Free): Auto-enrolled for all Jio users
- Plus (₹499–₹699/year): Cashback + OTT + delivery benefits
- Max (₹1499–₹1999/year): Family pooling + highest rewards

### 2. Unified Rewards Wallet
- Earn points across recharge, shopping, OTT, and payments
- Redeem across any Jio platform
- Real-time point tracking

### 3. Cross-Platform Ecosystem
- MyJio integration as entry point
- Seamless SSO across apps
- Offline + online redemption support

### 4. Family Pooling (Max Tier)
- Up to 5 members
- Shared wallet and benefits

---

## Expected Impact
- +20% cross-platform usage
- +15% increase in ARPU
- +30% repeat purchase rate
- +40% Jio SIM base enrolled in Lite tier
